Odyssey Mission - Debriefing

Today, we remember Odyssey I as a pioneer in early solar-system probes, a precursor to the more notable, and successful Voyager deep-space probes. A landmark achievement for scientists and engineers everywhere. The Odyssey Mission will always be remembered for its continued efforts at propagating discovery and advancement. Despite the controversies surrounding it, we celebrate it’s continued service to our community of scientists, and learn from it’s errors so we can better our future projects together.

Today, we continue to compare the data that Odyssey I collected on its journey to that of the Voyager probes, as well as any future programs that will be coming up.
